Who?

Coimbatore Smiley Project is a registered NGO founded and run by students for the purpose of educating less fortunate children.

grade

What?

Coimbatore Smiley Project provides a platform for students from different institutions in Coimbatore to support and educate children.

grade

But Why?

We believe that the best gift for the children is education, as it gives them the ability and the freedom to stand up for themselves.

About

grade

How we do it?

Coimbatore Smiley Project aims to connect all the students in Coimbatore for a collective social responsibility. The NGO follows the structure of a Public company, i.e. the NGO is not led by one particular person but by a ‘Head Committee’ led by a Chairperson. This Head Committee is composed of student representatives of different colleges in the city. All discussions and decisions are made by this committee led by the Chairperson. This ensures equal voice among the members, a channel for all volunteers from different colleges to contribute to the NGO, and represent the student community as a whole for positive impact.
